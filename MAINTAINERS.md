# Maintainers

Maintainers review and merge contributions, triage issues, and cut releases. See
[GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) for roles and how to become one.

| Maintainer | Role | Areas |
|------------|------|-------|
| [@rajanbor](https://github.com/rajanbor) | Lead maintainer | Everything |

## Want to help maintain MAP?

We add maintainers as the project grows. If you've contributed quality patterns,
reviews, or tooling and want to help maintain a category or area, say so in a
[Discussion](https://github.com/rajanbor/map/discussions) or open an issue. See the
"Becoming a maintainer" section in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).
